Glossary: Terms & Meanings

Term Meaning Notes
Bet Rebate Cashback based on turnover (total player activity). This bonus rewards activity. It's calculated on the total sum of all bets made (turnover), regardless of whether they were wins or losses. It functions as a loyalty reward.
Loss Rebate Conventional cashback based on net losses. This bonus refunds a percentage of a player's net loss over a period. Net loss is typically calculated as (Total Bets - Total Wins).
Rollover / Wagering Requirement A condition to bet a certain amount before withdrawing bonus funds. It's a multiplier applied to the bonus or bonus + deposit amount. A 10x rollover on a â‚±100 bonus means you must bet â‚±1,000 before withdrawal.
Turnover / Bet Amount The total sum of money wagered on games. It doesn't matter if the bets result in wins or losses. Ten bets of â‚±10 each result in a turnover of â‚±100. This is the basis for calculating a Bet Rebate.
KYC / Verify Account "Know Your Customer" is a mandatory identity verification process. Usually involves submitting documents like an ID and proof of address. It's a standard security measure required for most promotions and withdrawals.
JILI Games A specific provider/developer of online casino games. JILI is a popular game provider in Asia known for creating slots (like SUPER ACE), fishing games, and various arcade-style titles. When a promotion states "except JILI Games," it means any bets placed on games from this specific developer will not count towards that promotion's requirements.
Fish/Fishing Games An arcade-style casino game where players bet on shots to "capture" various sea creatures swimming across the screen. Each creature has a different value, and rarer ones offer bigger prizes. It combines elements of skill (aiming) and luck. This genre is often multiplayer.
Sabong / Cockfight A traditional blood sport where two roosters, fitted with metal spurs, fight in an arena. In the context of online casinos, it refers to placing bets on live-streamed cockfighting events, a popular practice in the Philippines.
HS Slot Refers to slot games from the provider Hacksaw Gaming. "HS" is the abbreviation for Hacksaw.
PP Slot Refers to slot games from the provider Pragmatic Play. "PP" is the abbreviation for Pragmatic Play, a major developer of casino games.
